Summary of Recent Market Data

1.CBRT Balance Sheet

Following charts summarize cbrt balance sheet evolutions

- Total Asset & Foreign Assets

Increase in percent of foreign assets to total assets indicates that CBRT is funding the market mostly through FX operations(swaps etc.)

- State Deposits

Increase in state deposits on CBRT might indicate that state debt issuances are increasing or taxes/payments are placed for a time being

- Gross and Net Reserves

Net reserves are calculated by excluding local and international swaps from gross reserves
